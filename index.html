KEY BACTERIA
🦠 Rhizobium - Fixes N₂ in root nodules
🦠 Nitrosomonas - NH₄⁺ → NO₂⁻
🦠 Nitrobacter - NO₂⁻ → NO₃⁻
🦠 Pseudomonas - Denitrifies NO₃⁻
🎮 GAMEPLAY TIPS
⚡ FIX - Convert atmospheric N₂ → NH₄⁺ (basic plant food)
🍂 DECOMPOSE - Break down organic matter → NH₄⁺
⚗ NITRIFY 1→2 - NH₄⁺ → NO₂⁻ → NO₃⁻. NO₃⁻ gives +50% FEED BONUS!
🌱 FEED - Give nitrogen to your plant! NO₃⁻ is 50% better than NH₄⁺!
♻ DENITRIFY - Reduce NO₃⁻ when high to prevent leaching & toxicity
⚠ WATCH OUT - NO₂⁻ is toxic! N₂O is a greenhouse gas! Leaching pollutes water!
